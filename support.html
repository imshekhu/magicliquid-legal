Thanks for playing! Here’s how the game works and how to get help.

How to play

  • Tap a bottle to pick it up, then tap another to pour.
  • Liquid only pours onto an empty bottle or matching color, and same‑colored liquid pours together.
  • Sort every bottle so each holds a single color to win the level.
  • Use undo (“backtracks”) to take back a move — you earn more by clearing levels cleanly.
