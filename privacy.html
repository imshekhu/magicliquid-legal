Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This game does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It works entirely offline.

What we collect

Nothing. The game has no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party software development kits. It never connects to the internet.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress — current level, your settings (such as the color‑blind option), and your earned “backtrack” count — is saved locally on your device only, using the operating system’s standard storage. This information never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Device motion

The game reads your device’s tilt/motion solely to create a subtle on‑screen visual (parallax) effect. This data is used only in the moment, is never stored and never transmitted, and is not used to identify you.

Children’s privacy

Because the game collects no data at all, it does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.
